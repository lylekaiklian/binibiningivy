About Ivy

Ivy is a budding photographer who dreams of venturing into weddings, portraiture and events. She fell in love with the craft when she learned the fundamentals of photography (in general) and photojournalism (in particular). She also started out and honed her skills in a vast studio called the streets, alongside finishing a degree in Business Management. She always ensures that in her every capture there is a story - in the most candid and natural way, that is.
